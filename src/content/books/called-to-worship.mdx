## Summary

Called to Worship grounds worship in Scripture from Genesis to Revelation and reframes it as a covenant response to God's initiating call. Vernon Whaley shows that worship is more than music style or church programming; it is a whole-life posture of reverence, love, obedience, and delight in who God is and what He has done. The book traces key biblical patterns: God reveals Himself, His people respond, and their lives are reshaped in holiness and mission. Read this if you want theological clarity and practical categories for personal and corporate worship. Implement it by building undistracted time with God, preparing your heart before gathered worship, and aligning daily choices with worship as surrender, not performance. This book helps move worship from routine attendance to transformed allegiance.
